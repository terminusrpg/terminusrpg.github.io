Terminus

A Minecraft MMORPG

About

Terminus RPG is an upcoming, open-world Minecraft MMORPG for Java Edition. - A fully custom, hand-built map filled with villages, ruins and mysterious places to explore. - Find and craft unique items to help you on your adventure. - Fight custom mobs and bosses. - Complete quests to earn XP and level up. - Battle your way through dungeons and raids to earn rare and epic gear.
