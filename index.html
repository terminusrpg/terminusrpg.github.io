Classes

Choose from 6 classes to start your adventure.

Available classes on release are Warrior, Hunter, Rogue, Mage, Priest and Shaman. Each class has four spells unique to them which you can cast by clicking your mouse buttons in a certain sequence.

The World

A fully custom world for you to explore.

Explore an expansive fully custom built landscape (no world painter) thousands of blocks wide. All over the world you will find towns, villages, ruins, caves and life. Be sure to check out the dynmap for a better look.

Items

Hundreds of custom items help immerse you in this world.

Throughout your adventures you'll come across hundreds of custom items, armour and weapons. You'll unlock new crafting recipes, new cooking recipes and find new ingredients for brewing. You may even come across a legendary item.

Creatures

Who knows what mysterious creatures await you...

Terminus RPG features custom mobs across the whole map. As you enter higher level areas the mobs will be stronger to keep up the challenge.

Quests

No RPG is complete without quests.

There are many, many quests throughout the world that will give you experience and items to help you reach the endgame. You will also have daily quests to give you a little bit extra gold and experience. These reset every day.

Dungeons

An extra challenge...

As you level up you will come across dungeons to explore. These dungeons provide a harder gameplay experience than the outside world. If you make it all the way through a dungeon and kill the boss you will find a chest. This chest can contain rare armour and weapons to make the challenge worthwhile.
