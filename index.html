About

Terminus RPG is an upcoming, open-world Minecraft MMORPG for Java Edition. learn more below.
Classes
Choose from six different classes each with unique spells.
The Isle of Termina
Explore a fully custom, hand-built map filled with villages, ruins and mysterious places to explore.
Items
Find and craft unique items to help you on your adventure.
Creatures
Fight custom mobs and bosses as you explore the world.
Quests
Complete quests to earn XP and level up.
Dungeons
Battle your way through dungeons and raids to earn rare and epic gear.

Roadmap

A timeline of things to come.

  • Early 2023

    A final closed beta

    One last beta to iron out any last bugs before release. Players will have the option to boost themselves to a higher level to test out later content that they would otherwise miss. Unfortunately player progress will be reset when the beta ends but any participants will earn an ingame title upon full release.

  • Later in 2023

    First release

    The server will be open to all. We hope the initial opening will be as bug free as possible and that you can enjoy what has taken a long time to make.

  • Mid 2023

    Raids

    As soon as the server opens and any immediate bugs have been fixed, we will begin work on the first end game raid of which players can vote and decide what that will be. This will give players plenty of time to level up to max level before the raid is released. We anticipate at least a month from release until there are enough players at level 50 to complete a raid.

  • Late 2023

    Events

    Depending on what the next major holiday is after release will dictate the first event. So whether that's Halloween or Christmas be sure to log in and get exclusive rewards, items, titles and mounts during the event weeks.
